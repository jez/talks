React.js: A Crash Course

January 28, 2016

At CMU, I was a part of a student organization called ScottyLabs. Among lots of other things, the thing we were most well-known for was as the group that put on TartanHacks, a collegiate hackathon open to CMU students in early February.

To provide the best possible experience for participants at TartanHacks, we would run a number of events throughout the year to help them learn skills that are useful at hackathons. In particular, at one called Crash Course I gave a talk to introduce participants to the essentials of React.js.
