Tmux & Other Tools

October 21, 2016

This informal tech talk demos some of my favorite interactive command line programs. I gave it as a part of the course 15-131 Great Practical Ideas for Computer Scientists at CMU.

In particular, we explore package managers, tmux, fzf, and ag. These programs blew my mind the first time I discovered them, and I enjoy being able to share them with others.
